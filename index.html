Hi, I'm Carolyn.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects, or advancing my coding skills, I can be found traveling, reading books, and lending a hand on my family's cattle ranch.

I was born in Calgary, Alberta but was raised an hour from the city on a cattle ranch. We traveled all over the province with our show cattle, with the main event being the Calgary Stampede!

Although my family roots at the ranch run deep, I've always had an interest in exploring the world. A few of my favourite places I've visited are South Africa, New Zealand, and Iceland.

Growing up in the prairies, I had very limited exposure to large bodies of water. So I was facinated by the ocean and developed a keen interest in Great White sharks. My biggest bucket list item was to cage dive, which I did in South Africa!
